# Marcus Hale

**Role:** Operator; principal of Sable Meridian Strategic Consulting
**Affiliation:** Sable Meridian (Zug); formerly Balkans arms trade, Congo mineral extraction
**Appears in:** Referenced in [The Chartered Flesh](../stories/the-chartered-flesh.md); documented in UET-1 Wiki PDF
**On-page presence:** Never directly appears in any story

---

## Voice

He has no documented voice. He is described through his effects. What he set in motion is visible; he is not.

---

## Want / Need / Lie / Ghost

**Want:** The Stolar keys — $940M in Bitcoin. Control of the Stolar Key Extraction operation.

**Need / Lie / Ghost:** Not established in any story. He is a structural function, not a character with interiority.

---

## Background (from UET-1 Wiki PDF)

- Born approximately 1963
- Arms trade, Balkans, through the 1990s
- Mineral extraction operations, Congo, early 2000s
- 2022: Under sanctions for evasion
- Current: Principal of Sable Meridian, registered Zug, Switzerland
- Sable Meridian has approximately 12 employees listed as "strategic consulting"
- Operates through intermediaries; no direct presence on the UET-1 is documented

---

## Operations (confirmed)

- **Stolar Key Extraction** — planned and benefited from; operationally executed through Alex and the BRX-90 compound; keys received in Zug via compromised Car 7 relay; see [BRX-90](../world/brx-90.md)

---

## Relationships

- **Alex** — recruiter; recruits Rowan on Hale's behalf; intermediary layer between Hale and operations
- **Rowan** — unwitting instrument; see [Rowan](rowan.md)
- **Sable Meridian** — his organization; see [Factions](../world/factions.md)

---

## Canon Ambiguities

- The UET-1 Wiki PDF entry on Hale lists him as a known figure; his involvement in the BRX-90 operation is documented but his connection to other stories (The Covenant, The Lights, Mirage Handler) is unspecified.
- Whether he is connected to the Car 7 attack as described in the UET-1 Wiki PDF is unresolved — the attack and the Stolar extraction may or may not be the same event.
