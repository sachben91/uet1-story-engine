# The Chartered Flesh

**Status:** Completed, canonical
**Source:** `wiki/sources/The Chartered Flesh .pdf`
**POV:** Rowan (close third-person)

---

## Summary

Rowan, a somatic self-optimization specialist and gray-market peptide courier, is recruited by a man called Alex to carry an experimental compound — BRX-90 — across the UET-1 for four paying passengers. She believes she is delivering a metabolic-enhancement product. She is not. BRX-90 encodes the cryptographic keys to Stolar's $940M Bitcoin holdings into the connective tissue of whoever carries it. The decontamination array in Car 7 reads those keys as they pass through. A compromised relay intercepts the telemetry. Marcus Hale's operatives receive the keys in Zug. Rowan and the four friends never know any of this happened. The price she pays is invisible to her — and that invisibility is its own cost.

## Arc Positions (Story Circle)

**Rowan:**
- **YOU:** Somatic autonomy specialist. The body is the only document that can't be forged.
- **NEED:** To keep her body sovereign — to optimize it on her own terms, for herself.
- **GO:** Accepts the job. Puts herself on the train with BRX-90 in her system.
- **SEARCH:** Does the work. Administers the compound. Watches the friends.
- **FIND:** The "Class 2 metabolic variance" notification. Something happened in Car 7 that wasn't in the brief.
- **TAKE:** She doesn't understand what she found — yet. She takes the severance (30 vials of real compound).
- **RETURN:** She holds the vials. "This one actually works."
- **CHANGE:** Unclear if she fully understands the cost. The recurring dreams about Car 7 — being read as a surface — suggest the body absorbs what the mind hasn't yet processed.

## Tensions Active

**Primary:** [Body as Instrument vs. Body as Self](../concepts/tensions.md#3-body-as-instrument-vs-body-as-self) — Rowan's entire project is somatic autonomy. Precisely because her body was optimized, it became the perfect instrument for someone else's purposes.

**Secondary:** [Craft vs. Complicity](../concepts/tensions.md#9-craft-vs-complicity) — Rowan's expertise is real. She doesn't ask what the compound is actually for. Her not-asking enables the operation as much as her skills do.

**Also running:** [Visibility vs. Erasure](../concepts/tensions.md#2-visibility-vs-erasure) — BRX-90 makes the body's contents legible to ECOROUTE infrastructure in a way Rowan cannot see or consent to.

## New Canon Introduced

- **[BRX-90](../world/brx-90.md)** — nanoparticle compound encoding cryptographic keys into connective tissue; readable by decontamination array
- **The Stolar Key Extraction** — operation to extract $940M Bitcoin keys from Stolar's holdings using BRX-90 carriers on UET-1
- **Car 7 decontamination array** — reads biometric data during gauge transition; has a compromised relay that routes to Hale's operatives in Zug
- **Class 2 metabolic variance** — biometric classification assigned to Rowan post-extraction; her body carries a record she cannot read

## Open Questions / Ambiguities

- Do the four friends ever discover what happened to them? The story ends without resolution.

- Is the compromised Car 7 relay the same vulnerability referenced in the UET-1 Wiki PDF under "Known Vulnerabilities"? The wiki PDF describes the Car 7 attack without naming BRX-90 specifically.
