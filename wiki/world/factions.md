# Factions and Powers

**Category:** World — Political Entities
**Source:** `wiki/sources/UET-1 Wiki.pdf`, `skills/story-bible/references/bible.json`

---

## The Fiduciary States

**Type:** Institutional/political bloc
**Character:** The enforcement pole. The Fiduciary States built and maintain much of the international compliance infrastructure — ECOROUTE's architecture, the biometric identity system, the enforcement protocols. They frame everything as accountability and rule of law. They use it to capture.

**Key figures:**
- [The Spy](../characters/the-spy.md) — Fiduciary States intelligence officer
- Captain Eriksson — cited in the UET-1 universe as embodying institutional doctrine; "the train does not stop"

**Appears in:** [Ghost Inside the Light](../stories/ghost-inside-the-light.md), referenced throughout

---

## The Telluric Empire

**Type:** Institutional/political bloc
**Character:** The other empire. Competes with the Fiduciary States for control of the UET-1 corridor and its infrastructure. Also wants Lin Yuan Exact. Also speaks the language of principle while meaning something else. From Felix's perspective (in *The Headless Empire*), both empires are interchangeable in their methods — they differ in flag, not function.

**Appears in:** [The Headless Empire](../stories/the-headless-empire.md) (both empires call Felix post-Lisbon)

---

## The Neutral Forge (Exact Yield Semiconductors)

**Type:** Corporation / neutral party
**Character:** Manufactures semiconductors for all clients. The Oath of Making: fabricate but never architect, hold secrets but never use them, serve all but favour none. Trust is the true product; chips are its evidence. Genuine neutrality — which made it indispensable to both empires and threatened by both.

**Key figures:**
- [Lin Yuan Exact](../characters/lin-yuan-exact.md) — founder, CEO, disappeared
- [Felix Lim](../characters/felix-lim.md) — Chief of Staff

**Appears in:** [The Headless Empire](../stories/the-headless-empire.md)

---

## Sable Meridian

**Type:** Private intelligence / strategic consulting firm
**Location:** Zug, Switzerland
**Employees:** Approximately 12 (listed as "strategic consulting")
**Character:** The gray-market operations layer. Deniable, effective, expensive. Executes operations that states and corporations want done without attribution.

**Key figures:**
- [Marcus Hale](../characters/marcus-hale.md) — principal

**Operations confirmed:**
- Stolar Key Extraction (via BRX-90 / Rowan)

**Appears in:** [The Chartered Flesh](../stories/the-chartered-flesh.md) (as beneficiary); UET-1 Wiki PDF

---

## Corvin Field Services

**Type:** Contractor organization
**Character:** Uses installation art and infrastructure work as cover for reconnaissance. Employs [The Carrier](../characters/the-carrier.md) for light installations in the Central Asian steppe. The installations are real and beautiful. The reconnaissance infrastructure embedded in them is also real.

**Appears in:** [Ghost Inside the Light](../stories/ghost-inside-the-light.md)

**Ambiguities:** Corvin's affiliations are not specified. It may operate for the Fiduciary States, the Telluric Empire, Sable Meridian, or independently. The story does not specify.

---

## Strait Country

**Type:** Jurisdiction / neutral fiscal zone
**Character:** The neutral zone where the Covenant operates. Its fiscal ledger hosts the Covenant's self-executing contracts. Neither empire fully controls it.

**Appears in:** [The Headless Empire](../stories/the-headless-empire.md) (Covenant embedded in its ledger; the Strait Country man in Lisbon)

---

## The Three Oracles

**Type:** Institutional systems / reference entities
**Character:** Referenced in `bible.json` as part of the visibility infrastructure — they produce the legal person, alongside ECOROUTE and bio-bands. Not yet featured in a story.

---

## Long Roots / Shunning

**Type:** Social/institutional mechanisms
**Character:** "Long Roots" is the mechanism of deep institutional memory — someone with Long Roots has a traceable history that cannot be erased. "Shunning" is the inverse — deliberate erasure from institutional record. Both are referenced in `bible.json`. Not yet featured prominently in any story.

---

## SWIFT/Murmur / Chain of Hands / Tirzepatide Trail

Referenced in `bible.json` as gray-market infrastructure:
- **SWIFT/Murmur** — financial messaging system with a gray-market layer
- **Chain of Hands** — physical courier network for deniable transport
- **Tirzepatide Trail** — GLP-1/peptide gray market; Rowan's trade environment

None have been featured prominently in a story yet.
