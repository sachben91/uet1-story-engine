# ECOROUTE

**Category:** Surveillance System
**Source:** `wiki/sources/UET-1 Wiki.pdf`, `skills/story-bible/references/bible.json`
**Implemented:** After the Car 7 / Black Wednesday attack

---

## What It Is

ECOROUTE is the biometric surveillance and passenger-tracking system integrated throughout the UET-1 infrastructure. It monitors all 2,347 passengers continuously, reconciles their presence against 17 jurisdictional databases, and maintains behavioral scoring with retroactive capability. It does not announce itself. It hums.

---

## How It Works

**Bio-bands:** Wristbands issued to all passengers at boarding. Continuously broadcast compliance status. Color-coded:

| Color | Status |
|-------|--------|
| Green | Compliant; full transit rights |
| Yellow | Advisory; minor flag |
| Amber | Restricted; requires escort |
| Deep amber | Significant restriction |
| Red | Detention pending |
| Black | Non-person status; referenced only in unverified accounts |

A black bio-band does not mean dead — it means the system has decided not to see you. The person still exists; their legal personhood does not.

**Passive sensor grid:** Embedded throughout the train. Motion, heat, biometric signature, location tracking. The decontamination array in Car 7 is the most intensive sensor cluster on the train — it reads biometrics at close range during gauge transitions.

**Data transmission:** Bio-band data is transmitted to ECOROUTE servers and to relevant jurisdictional authorities at each stop. The Car 7 relay compromise redirected some of this data to Marcus Hale's operatives in Zug.

**Retroactive behavioral scoring:** ECOROUTE can recalculate a passenger's score based on new information received after the fact. Being on the train is not a fixed moment — the record continues to be written.

**Decontamination procedure (Car 7):** A 9-step sequence all passengers undergo during gauge transitions. Step 7 involves the closest biometric reading. This is the step the Stolar Key Extraction exploited.

---

## The Surveillance Rule

ECOROUTE is weather, not revelation. It does not announce itself. It is simply present. If a surveillance system becomes the dramatic point of a scene, something has gone wrong.

In narrative terms: bio-bands appear in descriptions of what people are wearing; ECOROUTE data appears when it changes something; the decon procedure is physical sensation, not exposition.

## Ambiguities

- The "black bio-band" status is documented in the UET-1 Wiki PDF as referenced "only in unverified accounts." No story has yet depicted a character with a black bio-band.
- Who controls ECOROUTE? The UET-1 Wiki PDF implies joint jurisdictional authority, but the specific governance structure is not detailed.
- The Car 7 relay compromise — is it ongoing or was it a one-time operation? The Chartered Flesh implies it was specific to the Stolar extraction, but this is not confirmed.
