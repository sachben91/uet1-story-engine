## What It Is

The Universal Express Train 1. A transcontinental passenger rail line running 23,300 kilometers through 17 jurisdictions, from Hong Kong to Lisbon. Seven years in operation at story time. The most surveilled corridor in history — and also, because of its youth, a space where the normal enforcement infrastructure of international transit has not yet fully been built in.

"Twenty-three thousand, three hundred kilometers. One long room." — UET-1 Wiki PDF

---

## Technical Specifications

- **Total route length:** 23,300 km
- **Jurisdictions traversed:** 17
- **Average speed:** 225 km/h (scheduled)
- **Journey time:** Approximately 3 days Hong Kong → Lisbon (no delays)
- **Power:** Hybrid-electric architecture
- **Gauge transitions:** 5 different gauge widths along the route; transitions occur at jurisdictional borders; the train physically changes its wheelbase configuration

**Car configuration includes:**
- Observation car
- Dining car
- Business car (encrypted communications, privacy-filmed windows)
- Premium lounge
- Car 7 — decontamination array, gauge-change equipment, known vulnerability site
- Casino Car ("Loss and Gain") — documented in UET-1 Wiki PDF

## Jurisdictional Status

The UET-1 operates under a perpetual legal fiction: the train is simultaneously in transit through all jurisdictions and subject to none of them fully. Stopping constitutes a jurisdictional event — the train becoming subject to the laws of wherever it has stopped. Movement is the train's primary legal protection.

Security personnel on the train include overlapping authorities from multiple jurisdictions. Their mandate areas are not always clearly delineated.

---

## Car 7

The gauge-change car. Contains the decontamination array — a bio-surveillance system that reads biometric data from passengers routed through it during gauge transitions. Passage through Car 7 is not universal: passengers are routed there when flagged by ECOROUTE for a biometric irregularity or compliance issue. This is also the site of the Stolar Key Extraction vulnerability: the decontamination array's relay was compromised, routing telemetry to Marcus Hale's operatives in Zug when the activation signal was broadcast.

The UET-1 Wiki PDF classifies this as a "Known Vulnerability" under the heading of the Car 7 attack.

## The Train as Setting

The train is not neutral infrastructure. It is:
- A space where normal behavioral constraints are loosened but not removed
- 23,300 km of contiguous surveillance under [ECOROUTE](ecoroute.md)
- A gap in the airport enforcement architecture (Lin Yuan Exact's observation: "The Fiduciary State learned long ago that international airports are chokepoints")
- A pressure chamber: distance is not safety; everyone on the corridor is in the same sealed space

The ungoverned atmosphere is real and documented: the observation car, the business car with privacy film, the children's barricade in the corridors. These are not anomalies — they are the train functioning as designed.
